This is Dunosis

Dunosis was founded by two passionate innovators bridging two vibrant cities: Chicago, Illinois and Santa Cruz, Bolivia. Wesley Ordoñez brings a wealth of experience in technology and engineering, while Tayo Zabala specializes in marketing and business strategy. Together, we combine global perspectives and local expertise to deliver impactful results for businesses of all sizes. Between us, we speak four languages—English, Spanish, Portuguese, and French—allowing us to connect with clients from diverse backgrounds. We’re ready to help you achieve your goals, no matter where you are and committed to turning your ideas into powerful solutions.

Our Mission

We aim to empower businesses and communities through exceptional solutions while creating opportunities for talented Bolivians. By delivering expertly crafted websites, actionable data insights, and tailored tech consulting, we help our clients thrive in an increasingly digital world. We’re committed to professionalism, flexibility, and trust. At the same time, we strive to economically support Bolivian talent, provide real-world experience with American companies, and open pathways for cultural and professional exchange.
