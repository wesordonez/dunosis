Feast

FEAST is an emerging food delivery platform offering a fresh alternative to traditional apps like Uber Eats and DoorDash. Focused on local partnerships and streamlined service, FEAST needed a modern, dynamic landing page to highlight its three core verticals: customers ordering food, restaurants managing their menus, and couriers delivering orders. The goal was to create a visually engaging experience that clearly speaks to each audience while keeping everything simple, intuitive, and conversion-driven.

What We Provided

We designed and developed a custom landing page that dynamically presents content for FEAST’s three business pillars. Our work included strategic layout planning, visual design, and user experience optimization to drive engagement for each user type. We also built and integrated a custom form that sends real-time submissions directly to the client’s email, ensuring no lead gets missed. All content was designed to be easily scalable as the app continues to grow.

Results

Our landing page successfully set the tone for FEAST’s brand identity and digital presence. The dynamic content approach created clarity for each user group, helping visitors immediately find their path, while the custom form ensured seamless lead generation. As a result, FEAST saw a sharp increase in inquiries from restaurants, couriers, and customers during their launch phase, giving the app the strong start it needed to build momentum.
