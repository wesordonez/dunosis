Colonia Jardín

Colonia Jardín is a real estate development located on the east side of Santa Cruz, Bolivia, in a growing residential zone. The project offered land lots with flexible payment options—including one-time payments and monthly installment plans—making it accessible to a wide range of buyers. A portion of the project included a gated neighborhood, adding security and value to the offering. Its appeal lay in both its location and its practical, community-centered approach to home ownership.

What We Provided

Our work with Colonia Jardín focused heavily on visual communication and promotional strategy. We delivered a range of graphic design materials, managed their social media presence, and created targeted campaigns tailored to the client’s evolving needs. This included everything from digital ads and branded posts to physical signage and banners. We worked closely with their sales goals and budget to ensure every piece of design work had strategic purpose and impact.
