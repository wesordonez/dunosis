Colonia Jardín

The Puerto Rican Cultural Center (PRCC) needed a solution to streamline their business advising consultations and gather actionable insights. We developed a custom Google Form linked to an automated data dashboard, enabling the Business Initiatives Department to easily input, track, and analyze their business advising efforts. This project empowered the PRCC to provide better support for the local community by using data to drive impactful decisions.

What We Provided

Our solution for PRCC included a seamlessly integrated Google Form and an automated data dashboard. The Google Form allowed team members to efficiently input consultation data, while the dashboard dynamically visualized key insights such as consultation counts, advisor performance, and program impact metrics. This streamlined system enhanced their ability to track progress, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to support local businesses effectively.

Results

The PRCC’s new system transformed their approach to business advising. By automating data collection and visualization, they drastically reduced manual work and improved operational efficiency. The integrated dashboard provided immediate access to critical metrics, empowering the team to focus on supporting businesses rather than struggling with data management.

This solution not only increased transparency and accountability but also enhanced the PRCC’s ability to demonstrate program impact to stakeholders. The streamlined system positioned the PRCC to better serve its mission of uplifting local businesses and fostering community growth.

With this data-driven approach, the PRCC continues to empower entrepreneurs and small business owners, creating lasting positive change in their community.
