Maintenance and Support

Keeping your website secure, updated, and running smoothly is critical to your success. At Dunosis, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to ensure your website stays optimized long after launch. What We Provide

Our maintenance and support packages are designed to give you peace of mind and keep your website operating at its best. After your first year of complimentary support, you can choose a plan that suits your needs, whether it’s basic upkeep or full-scale management.

Our services include regular database backups, server maintenance, domain renewals, and website content management. Depending on your package, you’ll also have access to on-call support, with options ranging from standard business hours to 24/7 coverage. Need to upload new photos or update website text? We include monthly hours for content updates as part of your plan.
